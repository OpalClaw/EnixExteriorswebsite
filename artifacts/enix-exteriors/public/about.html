OUR STORY

BUILT ON
QUALITY AND
INTEGRITY

Enix Exteriors was founded with one mission: deliver the highest quality roofing and exterior work in Tennessee, at a fair price, backed by a guarantee you can trust.

Based in Knoxville at 5992 Bearden View Ln, our team has grown to serve communities from Memphis to Johnson City, Nashville to Chattanooga. No subcontractors, no shortcuts — just our own licensed crews doing the work right.

We specialize in commercial roofing, residential roofing, siding, gutters, and storm damage restoration. When you hire Enix Exteriors, you get one team responsible for everything — and one warranty that covers it all.
